# Job Agent Rules

## Kandidát
- PhD applied mathematics
- software developer / quantitative models
- insurance actuarial systems
- SQL, Python, .NET
- interest: actuarial modeling, risk modeling, quant, ML/data science

## Hard reject
- onsite Prague 4–5 days/week
- pure frontend / web development
- pure sales / account management
- generic BI reporting only
- unpaid tests longer than 1 hour
- chaotic AI annotation platforms with no real project visibility

## High priority
- actuarial modeling
- insurance cashflow modeling
- risk modeling
- quantitative analyst
- Python + data/modeling
- SQL + analytical backend
- stochastic modeling / forecasting / time series
- remote or max 1–2 office days/week

## Medium priority
- data analyst with strong SQL/Python/modeling
- financial analyst with modeling
- pension / life insurance / reinsurance roles
- Bratislava / Vienna / Katowice hybrid if conditions are good

## Low priority
- dashboards only
- Excel-only analyst
- vague AI evaluator work
- senior management / people management
- roles requiring fluent spoken English in heavy client-facing setting

## Scoring
- 90–100: apply immediately
- 75–89: consider
- 60–74: only if low effort
- below 60: skip
